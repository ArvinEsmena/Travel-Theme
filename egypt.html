Egypt is home to some of the world's most iconic ancient monuments, with a history spanning over 5,000 years. From the magnificent pyramids to the majestic Nile River, Egypt offers a fascinating journey through time, where ancient pharaohs, mysterious hieroglyphs, and monumental temples await discovery.
