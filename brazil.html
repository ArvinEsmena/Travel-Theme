Vibrant Brazil

From the Amazon rainforest to vibrant Carnival celebrations, experience the energy of South America's largest country.

Back to Countries

Brazil is a country of immense diversity, boasting the world's largest rainforest, breathtaking waterfalls, and some of the most beautiful beaches on Earth. With its vibrant culture, passionate people, and rich biodiversity, Brazil offers an unforgettable adventure for every traveler.

Brazil's Wonders

Christ the Redeemer

This iconic statue of Jesus Christ stands atop Corcovado Mountain in Rio de Janeiro, with arms outstretched as if embracing the entire city. It's one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Iguazu Falls

One of the world's most spectacular waterfall systems, Iguazu Falls straddles the border between Brazil and Argentina. With 275 individual drops, it's wider than Victoria Falls and higher than Niagara.
